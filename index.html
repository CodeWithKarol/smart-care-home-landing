Aging-in-Place Safety Tips

Knowledge is power. Learn how to create a safer home environment.

Home Safety Assessment

Identify and eliminate fall hazards like loose rugs, poor lighting, and cluttered pathways. SmartCare monitoring helps you notice when these safety measures need updating.

Medication Management

Establish routines for medication adherence. SmartCare tracks patterns to ensure doses aren't missed and alerts you to changes in behavior.

Nutrition & Hydration

Monitor eating and drinking patterns to ensure proper nutrition. SmartCare learns normal routines and flags concerning changes.

Activity & Mobility

Encourage regular movement to maintain strength and independence. Track activity levels to ensure your loved one stays active.

Cognitive Stimulation

Maintain mental health through social engagement and mental activities. SmartCare can monitor social interactions and engagement patterns.

Sleep Quality

Ensure adequate, quality sleep for overall health. SmartCare monitors sleep patterns and alerts you to disruptions or concerns.
