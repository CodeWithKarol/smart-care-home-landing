Essential

Home Safety Assessment

Identify and eliminate fall hazards like loose rugs, poor lighting, and cluttered pathways. SmartCare monitoring helps you notice when safety measures need updating.

✓ Fall prevention ✓ Lighting audit ✓ Pathway clearance

Medication Management

Establish routines for medication adherence. SmartCare tracks patterns to ensure doses aren't missed and alerts you to changes in behavior.

✓ Dose tracking ✓ Pattern monitoring ✓ Alert system

Nutrition & Hydration

Monitor eating and drinking patterns to ensure proper nutrition. SmartCare learns normal routines and flags concerning changes.

✓ Pattern learning ✓ Anomaly detection ✓ Health insights
